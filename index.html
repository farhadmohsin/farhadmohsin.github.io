Research Interest

I'm doing research in preference aggregation and fair decision-making, specifically on designing fairer voting rules. I have been exploring ML and RL-based techniques to design economic mechanisms. Other broad interests include natural language processing, interpretable machine learning and multi-agent reinforcement learning
